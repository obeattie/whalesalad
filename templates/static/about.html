Hey there ladies and gents. My name is Michael Whalen, and I am a web developer. Since I was a kid, I have been fascinated and intrigued by technology. For the past few years I've been working hard at becoming a stellar web designer, and have been having an awesome time. Looking back at where I have come from, where I am now, and the what the future looks like future is a daunting and exciting thought.

Work

Right now I am working full time at a technology company in downtown Honolulu called Blue Lava. I was working on their Kindfish product, but we've shifted focus and are working on a brand new, totally cool site that (we're hoping) will revolutionize the way people organize their photos. I love the team at Blue Lava, and unlike most Americans, enjoy heading in to work each day!

SchoolRack

My true passion lies with SchoolRack. My team and I were contracted a few months ago to redesign and redevelop SchoolRack, a website dedicated to teachers. SchoolRack aims to help bring students and teachers closer together outside of the classroom by giving teachers the ability to post information online, upload files for download, and manage mailing lists for parents and students. We're actively working on the website and are working around the clock to release the 2nd phase of the new design by the end of August. For those wondering, we built SchoolRack in my new love affair, Django.

Devskunk

Back to the team, Devskunk. Devskunk was born at the beginning of this year out of frustration caused by various projects we were working on. We're composed of Jason Skicewicz, an all-star system architect with years of experience with various back end technologies, Nesan Waran, another amazingly talented system architect with vast experience and specialty in ui design, and of course myself, the creative interface designer specializing in XHTML and CSS. We've got a ton of ideas, and a ton of talent, but we're lacking the funds and the time to get ourselves off the ground right now. Hopefully we'll have our own Devskunk website up real soon.
